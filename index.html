HOME

SERVERS, WIFI


Server

A server uses a wired connection that connects your server to your network. Network cables are connected to your device and the other end is connceted to the server/router. The router is connected to a device that has broadband access to allow internet data to be sent.

Wifi Extension

Other extension wifi servers are used to expand a devices network's reach, adding capacity in areas of the home where the original router does not. This is connected to primary source of internet and is used as another source to connect to.

Wireless Router

Wifi router's are a common way of accesing the internet at home. A wireless router recieves the internet's data through the phone line and converts that data into radio signals. These signals are sent to your computer or network device and transmitted into internet data.

UNIVERISTY

CABLES, DSL


Cables

Network Ethernet crossover cables are used to connect devices together. These cables carry the broadband signals between your modem, router and computer. Ethernet cables accomodate different speeds and levels of internet service.

DSL

Stands for Digital Subscriber Line, DSL uses telephone line connected to the home which allows it to be delivered at the same time as a landline. This also allows users to make calls whilst internet surfing.
